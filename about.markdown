# Alistair Tran

Hello!

I’m a game developer and leader who has been working on games since middle school. Over the past 7+ years, I’ve worked on tiny indie narrative projects to sprawling 200+ people teams. Most of my experience is within Unity, however I’ve worked in Godot and Game Maker before.

I’ve always been the kind of person to attack things head on. My game development journey started after seeing a youtube video about a game jam. I jumped in head first with no programming or game design experience, and through that mindset I was able to learn so much. I’ve kept at it! I’m learning Godot right now, as well as making this website using HTML….I love learning new things, getting lost, and finding my way home.

I’ve worn many different hats over the years. Whether it be solely game design, programming, sound design, narrative design, or even video editing, interdisciplinary work is the place I want to be. I’ve learned so much about making games over the years, and it has helped so much in being able to create worlds and tell stories.

Outside of making games, I really like anime, board games, trading card games, music, bowling, volleyball, and most importantly…video games!
