An independently developed project, with a few contributors here and there.

*blu,* is a narrative game about a collection of games that don’t belong to its creator. The game takes place in a museum, where the player is guided through 12 mysterious games that were “found” on an abandoned laptop.

It serves as a social experiment on how much more an audience will **relate and bond** to a collection of art when separated from its author.

*blu,* not only presents the story found within the collection of games it presents, but also a story about the person who curated all of them together.
